# Migraine Printable Bundle

Thank you for using this printable bundle.

This bundle includes three migraine-specific printable products for tracking attacks, preparing for healthcare appointments, and reviewing possible patterns over time.

## Included Files

### Migraine Daily Symptom Tracker

- `migraine-daily-symptom-tracker-us-letter.pdf`
- `migraine-daily-symptom-tracker-a4.pdf`

This 2-page tracker is designed to help you record one migraine attack at a time, including timing, pain level, symptoms, possible context, relief, impact, medication/support notes, and questions for a healthcare provider.

### Migraine Doctor Visit Prep Pack

- `migraine-doctor-visit-prep-pack-us-letter.pdf`
- `migraine-doctor-visit-prep-pack-a4.pdf`

This 8-page pack is designed to help you organize notes before a migraine-related healthcare appointment, including frequency, symptom patterns, possible context, medication and treatment history, daily-life impact, questions, and follow-up notes.

### Migraine Pattern Workbook

- `migraine-pattern-workbook-us-letter.pdf`
- `migraine-pattern-workbook-a4.pdf`

This 10-page workbook is designed to help you organize possible context around migraine attacks, review patterns over time, and prepare clearer notes for a healthcare provider.

## Suggested Use

1. Start with the Daily Symptom Tracker when you want a quick log for a single migraine attack.
2. Use the Pattern Workbook when you want to review several attacks or context notes over time.
3. Use the Doctor Visit Prep Pack before a primary care, neurology, headache specialist, or follow-up appointment.
4. Print only the pages you need. You do not need to complete every section.
5. Keep completed pages together in a folder, binder, or clipboard if you plan to discuss them at an appointment.

## A Note on Possible Triggers

Migraine patterns can be complicated. These printables use "possible context" language because a tracker or workbook cannot prove what caused an attack. Some possible triggers may actually be early migraine symptoms, multiple factors may stack together, and some attacks may not have an obvious pattern.

This bundle is for organizing observations, not blaming yourself.

## Printing Tips

- Choose the US Letter files for 8.5 x 11 inch paper.
- Choose the A4 files for A4 paper.
- Print at 100% scale when possible.
- Black-and-white printing is fine.
- If anything prints too close to the edge, choose "Fit to printable area" in your printer settings.
- Reprint individual pages as needed.

## Important Medical Note

These printables are personal tracking and organization tools. They are not medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always talk with a qualified healthcare professional about symptoms, medications, or care decisions.

These printables cannot prove what caused a migraine attack and should not be used to make medication, diet, or care changes without a qualified healthcare professional.

If symptoms feel new, sudden, severe, or concerning, contact a qualified healthcare professional or emergency services.
