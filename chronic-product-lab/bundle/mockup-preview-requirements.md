# Mockup and Preview Image Requirements

## Goal

Create preview images that help a buyer understand exactly what is included in the migraine printable bundle before purchase.

## Required Preview Images

1. Bundle cover preview
2. All included files preview
3. Daily Symptom Tracker page preview
4. Doctor Visit Prep Pack page preview
5. Pattern Workbook page preview
6. US Letter and A4 format note preview
7. Printing and use-case preview

## Image 1: Bundle Cover Preview

Show the bundle as a clean printable set with three visible product names:

- Migraine Daily Symptom Tracker
- Migraine Doctor Visit Prep Pack
- Migraine Pattern Workbook

Requirements:

- Use a bright, clean desk or paper mockup.
- Show printed pages, not a medical-device or clinic scene.
- Keep the look calm, practical, and readable.
- Avoid implying doctor endorsement.
- Avoid pill bottles, needles, dramatic pain imagery, or hospital styling.

## Image 2: All Included Files Preview

Show that the bundle includes:

- 3 printable products
- US Letter PDFs
- A4 PDFs
- 20 total printable pages per paper size

Requirements:

- Make the file list easy to scan.
- Use small PDF/file icons or page stack visuals.
- Do not show tiny unreadable full-page thumbnails as the only explanation.

## Image 3: Daily Symptom Tracker Preview

Show one or both pages from the 2-page tracker.

Highlight:

- Attack timing
- Symptoms
- Possible context
- Medication/support notes
- Appointment notes

Requirements:

- Use real page screenshots or exported page images.
- Keep text large enough to inspect on marketplace preview.
- Do not crop out the medical disclaimer if showing the full page.

## Image 4: Doctor Visit Prep Pack Preview

Show a few representative pages from the 8-page pack.

Highlight:

- Appointment snapshot
- Frequency and pattern summary
- Medication/treatment history
- Questions to ask
- Follow-up notes

Requirements:

- Make it clear this is for appointment organization.
- Avoid language that implies provider approval.
- Avoid stock photos of doctors or clinics unless the page itself remains the focus.

## Image 5: Pattern Workbook Preview

Show a few representative pages from the 10-page workbook.

Highlight:

- Attack log
- Sleep and schedule notes
- Food, caffeine, hydration, and alcohol notes
- Stress and sensory exposure notes
- Pattern review and doctor discussion page

Requirements:

- Use "possible context" or "pattern review" wording.
- Do not use "find your triggers" as a guaranteed promise.
- Avoid restrictive diet or cure-oriented framing.

## Image 6: US Letter and A4 Format Note

Show both supported paper sizes:

- US Letter, 8.5 x 11 inches
- A4

Requirements:

- Keep the message simple and visual.
- Mention that both formats are included.
- Do not imply a physical product is shipped.

## Image 7: Printing and Use-Case Preview

Show a printed page in a binder, folder, clipboard, or simple desk setting.

Requirements:

- Keep the page readable.
- Use neutral props such as a pen, folder, binder clip, or calendar.
- Avoid medical props that could make the product feel clinical or treatment-oriented.

## Visual Style

- Clean, calm, practical, and low-ink.
- Use a neutral background with a few soft accent colors.
- Keep contrast high enough for marketplace thumbnails.
- Use real PDF page screenshots whenever possible.
- Avoid cluttered collages.
- Avoid exaggerated wellness imagery.

## Copy Guardrails for Images

Safe phrases:

- "Migraine printable bundle"
- "Track symptoms and possible context"
- "Prepare notes for appointments"
- "US Letter and A4 included"
- "20 printable pages per paper size"
- "Digital download"

Avoid phrases:

- "Stop migraines"
- "Prevent attacks"
- "Find the cause"
- "Cure migraine"
- "Doctor approved"
- "Treatment planner"
- "Guaranteed trigger tracker"

## Technical Requirements

- Create square preview images for marketplace thumbnails.
- Recommended export size: 2000 x 2000 px.
- Keep important text within the center safe area.
- Use PNG or high-quality JPG.
- Include alt-text notes when uploading to a marketplace.

## Suggested Alt Text

- "Migraine printable bundle showing symptom tracker, doctor visit prep pack, and pattern workbook pages."
- "Preview of migraine tracker printable pages with symptom, possible context, and appointment note sections."
- "Migraine doctor visit prep printable pages for organizing appointment questions and follow-up notes."
- "Migraine pattern workbook preview with logs for possible context and pattern review."
