## Marketplace Risk Notes

Medical risk is low. The product avoids unsafe health claims and repeatedly frames itself as a personal tracking and organization tool.

Do not use phrases like:

- "Find your migraine triggers"
- "Prevent migraines"
- "Reduce migraine attacks"
- "Doctor-approved"

Safer phrases:

- "Record possible context"
- "Notice patterns over time"
- "Prepare notes for your healthcare provider"
- "Track what happened during an attack"
