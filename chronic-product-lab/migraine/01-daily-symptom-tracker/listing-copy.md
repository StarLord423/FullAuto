## Description

Track one migraine attack at a time with a printable log made for the details that are easy to forget later: aura, pain location, sensory symptoms, possible context, medication/support used, relief level, and notes to bring up with your healthcare provider.

Page 1 is a quick checkbox-heavy attack log. Page 2 gives you more room for appointment notes, patterns, medication/support details, and anything you want to remember later.

## Who This Is For

This tracker is for people with recurring migraine or chronic migraine who want a low-friction way to capture what happened during an attack. It may be especially helpful if you tend to forget details once the appointment finally comes around, or if phone apps feel like too much during light sensitivity or brain fog.

## What You Can Track

- Date, timing, and duration
- Pain intensity, location, and quality
- Migraine phase clues: prodrome, aura, headache, postdrome, or unsure
- Aura and neurological symptoms
- Nausea, light sensitivity, sound sensitivity, smell sensitivity, fatigue, and migraine hangover notes
- Possible context such as sleep changes, hydration, caffeine, weather, sensory exposure, stress, and cycle timing
- Medication or support used
- Relief level and daily-life impact
- Notes to share with a healthcare provider

## How to Use

1. Print both pages for each migraine attack you want to record, or print only page 1 when you need a quick log.
2. Use page 1 for fast checkboxes and short notes.
3. Use page 2 when you want more room for appointment prep or pattern notes.
4. Keep several completed trackers together if you want to discuss patterns at an appointment.
