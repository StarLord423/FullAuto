# Product Brief: Migraine Daily Symptom Tracker

## Condition

Migraine / Chronic Migraine

## Product Type

Two-page daily printable tracker.

## Target Buyer

Someone who gets recurring migraines and wants a simple way to remember symptoms, possible triggers, medications, and notes before talking with a healthcare provider.

## Goal

Create a clean two-page tracker that helps the buyer record one migraine episode without feeling overwhelmed. Page 1 should work as a quick attack log. Page 2 should provide expanded writing space for context, medication/support details, patterns, and appointment questions.

## Required Sections

Page 1: Quick Attack Log

- Date and day of week
- Start time and end time
- Pain location
- Pain intensity scale
- Migraine phase indicators
- Aura symptoms
- Associated symptoms
- Possible triggers
- Sleep
- Hydration
- Food/caffeine/alcohol notes
- Weather/barometric pressure notes
- Menstrual cycle/hormonal notes where relevant
- Medication/treatment used
- Relief level
- Impact on work/school/home
- Quick notes to mention to doctor

Page 2: Expanded Migraine Notes

- Date and attack label
- Provider or appointment notes
- Before-the-attack context with generous writing space
- During-the-attack notes with generous writing space
- Medication, support, and relief details
- Pattern notes
- Questions for healthcare provider
- Free notes section

## Format

- US Letter first
- A4 adaptable
- Two pages maximum
- Printable in black and white

## Quality Bar

This should feel like a migraine-specific tool, not a generic symptom tracker. It must remain exactly two pages in the US Letter PDF export and provide enough handwriting room on page 2 to justify the expanded format.
