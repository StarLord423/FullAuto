How to Use This Workbook

Use this to collect clues, not to prove one cause. Some attacks may not have an obvious pattern.
Fill in the pages that match your life. Write "unknown" or "not sure" when exact details are hard.
Review several entries together before drawing conclusions. Multiple small factors may stack together.
Bring the pattern review page to your healthcare provider if it helps the conversation.
