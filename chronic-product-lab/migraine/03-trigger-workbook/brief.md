# Product Brief: Migraine Pattern Workbook

## Condition

Migraine / Chronic Migraine

## Product Type

Eight to twelve page printable workbook.

## Target Buyer

Someone trying to notice patterns around migraine episodes and prepare clearer notes for a healthcare provider.

## Goal

Help the buyer track possible migraine context and patterns over time without implying that triggers are simple, universal, or guaranteed to be identifiable or controllable.

## Required Sections

- How to use the workbook
- Possible context category overview
- Sleep and schedule log
- Food, caffeine, and alcohol notes
- Stress and sensory exposure notes
- Weather and environment notes
- Hormonal/cycle notes where relevant
- Medication/treatment notes
- Pattern review page
- Doctor discussion page

## Quality Bar

The workbook should avoid oversimplifying migraine. It should support pattern noticing, not blame the buyer for their symptoms.
