## What This Proves

The local workflow can produce migraine-specific, medically cautious, printable products that pass strict review, receive human approval, and can be assembled into a local sales-test bundle.

This is not yet proof of marketplace demand.

## Safety Guardrails

Keep these in all future products, bundle files, previews, and listings:

- No diagnosis claims
- No treatment claims
- No prevention, cure, or guaranteed-outcome claims
- No fake doctor approval
- No medication-change or diet-change advice
- Use "possible context" and "patterns" instead of overconfident trigger claims
- Include medical disclaimers in printables, README files, and listing copy
