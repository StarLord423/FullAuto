# Migraine Doctor Visit Prep Pack

Thank you for using this printable.

This 8-page pack is designed to help you prepare notes for a migraine-related healthcare appointment. It can be used before a primary care, neurology, headache specialist, or follow-up visit.

## How to Use

1. Print the full pack, or print only the pages you need.
2. Fill in the appointment snapshot first so your main concerns are easy to find.
3. Use the frequency, symptom, possible context, treatment history, and impact pages to summarize what has been happening.
4. Write your top questions before the appointment.
5. Use the follow-up page during or right after the appointment to capture next steps.
6. Skip any section that is not useful for your visit.

## A Note on Possible Triggers

Migraine patterns can be complicated. The "possible context" page is there to help you organize observations for discussion, not to prove one single cause or blame yourself for an attack.

## Printing Tips

- Print at 100% scale when possible.
- Black-and-white printing is fine.
- If anything prints too close to the edge, choose "Fit to printable area" in your printer settings.
- Use a folder, binder, or clipboard to keep the pages together.
- Bring any recent migraine trackers, medication lists, or medical records if they are useful for your appointment.

## Important Medical Note

This printable is a personal tracking and organization tool. It is not medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always talk with a qualified healthcare professional about symptoms, medications, or care decisions.

If symptoms feel new, sudden, severe, or concerning, contact a qualified healthcare professional or emergency services.
