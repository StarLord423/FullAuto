# Product Brief: Migraine Doctor Visit Prep Pack

## Condition

Migraine / Chronic Migraine

## Product Type

Eight-page printable doctor appointment preparation pack.

## Target Buyer

Someone preparing for a primary care, neurology, headache specialist, or follow-up appointment who wants to communicate clearly about migraine patterns and impact.

## Goal

Help the buyer summarize migraine frequency, symptoms, possible context and patterns, treatments tried, appointment questions, and daily-life impact without making medical claims.

## Required Sections

- Appointment snapshot
- Migraine frequency summary
- Symptom pattern summary
- Possible context and pattern notes
- Medication/treatment history notes
- Impact on work, school, caregiving, and daily life
- Questions to ask
- What I need help with
- Follow-up notes

## Quality Bar

The pack should help a real person avoid blanking during an appointment.
